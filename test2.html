Ladder Rules
Enforced by honour, witnessed scoring, and this dashboard
  • Best of 3 legs – first to 2 wins.
  • Leg 3 only played if the first two legs are 1–1.
  • 45‑dart rule: after 45 darts each, decide by one dart nearest the bull.
  • Challenge rule: you may challenge up to 3 positions above you.
  • If the lower‑ranked player wins, they take the higher player’s position; others shift down.
  • New players always join at the bottom.
