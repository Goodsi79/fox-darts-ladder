Ladder Rules

  • Normal darts rules apply – straight in, double out.
  • Matches must be scored and witnessed by a third member of the ladder.
  • Players must declare to the scorer/witness that the match is a ladder match before starting.
  • Format: Best of 3 legs. Default 501 start, or 301 if both players agree.
  • 45‑dart rule: After each player has thrown 45 darts (15 visits), if neither has checked out, the leg is decided by one dart nearest the bull.
  • No remaining‑score comparison is used after 45 darts — nearest‑bull only.
  • Challenge rule: A player may challenge up to 3 positions above them in the ladder.
  • If the lower‑ranked player wins, they take the higher player’s position and everyone below shifts down one.
  • New players join at the bottom of the ladder.
  • The ladder runs continuously — inactive players naturally drift downward as others challenge above them.
These rules are enforced automatically by the dashboard where applicable.
