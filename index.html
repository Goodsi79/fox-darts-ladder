Ladder Rules
Enforced by honour, witnessed scoring, and this dashboard
  • Normal darts rules – straight in, double out.
  • Matches must be scored and witnessed by a third ladder member.
  • Declare the match as a ladder match before starting.
  • Format: Best of 3 legs. Default 501 start, or 301 if both agree.
  • 45‑dart rule: After 45 darts each (15 visits), if no checkout, decide by one dart nearest the bull.
  • No remaining‑score comparison after 45 darts — nearest‑bull only.
  • Challenge rule: You may challenge up to 3 positions above you.
  • If the lower‑ranked player wins, they take the higher player’s position; others shift down.
  • New players always join at the bottom.
  • Ladder runs continuously — inactivity naturally drops players down.
